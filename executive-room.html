Executive Rooms

Description

Our beautiful Executive Room measures an incredible 23 × 17 ft. The air conditioning provides a comfortable environment throughout your stay, providing ultimate comfort and luxury. With the included kettle, you may make a hot cup of tea or coffee whenever you like.

Each floor has a common couch area, which is ideal for socializing or unwinding. Stay entertained with the flat-screen TV, which has a range of channels, and connected with the complimentary WiFi in the room.

Enjoy a good night's sleep on the king-sized bed, which is designed to provide maximum comfort. Use the easy washing facilities to keep your clothing fresh and clean during your stay.

The Executive Room offers a range of basic facilities, including a wardrobe, bedside table, and a private bathroom with essential amenities. With its generous space and modern amenities, this room provides a perfect retreat for a comfortable and enjoyable stay.

Indulge in the spaciousness and luxury of the Executive Room, with thoughtful facilities like air conditioning, a kettle, a common couch area, a flat-screen TV, complimentary WiFi, a king bed, and convenient laundry services.
