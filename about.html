About Us

Welcome to Bliss Regency, your haven of luxury and comfort in the heart of Palakkad. We pride ourselves on providing exceptional hospitality and a wide range of accommodation options to suit every Business person’s and a traveler’s needs.

Indulge in regal elegance in our Royal Suite, offering a spacious retreat with a private couch area and a range of amenities including air conditioning, a flat-screen TV, and complimentary Wi-Fi. The suite is thoughtfully designed with a king-size bed, refrigerator, and access to our convenient laundry services.

For those seeking a touch of serenity, our Deluxe Rooms with balconies provide the perfect escape. Enjoy breath taking views of Palakkad's natural beauty from the comfort of your private balcony. Each room is equipped with air conditioning, a kettle, flat-screen TV, and free Wi-Fi, ensuring a delightful stay.

Our Executive Rooms offer a blend of comfort and sophistication. Relax in a plush king-size bed and enjoy amenities like air conditioning, a flat-screen TV, and complimentary Wi-Fi. In addition, all Royal Suite rooms feature a private couch area, allowing you to unwind in style.

Traveling with a companion? Our Executive Twin Bed rooms are perfect for you. Experience utmost comfort with two cozy beds and a range of facilities including air conditioning, a kettle, flat-screen TV, and free Wi-Fi. Enjoy the convenience of our basic amenities and make the most of your stay in Palakkad.

At Bliss Regency, we strive to create a memorable experience for our guests by combining modern comforts with personalized service. Whether you're visiting for business or leisure, our hotel is the perfect choice for a truly blissful stay in Palakkad.
