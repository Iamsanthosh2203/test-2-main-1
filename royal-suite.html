Royal Suite

Room Description

Our lavish Royal Suite, comprising a spacious 23 × 22 feet, combines luxury and comfort. Experience the height of luxury with the private sofa area, which is only available in our Royal Suite rooms and gives you a quiet area to unwind and rest.

Enjoy the perfect temperature year-round with the air conditioning system, ensuring your comfort during your entire stay. Savor a hot cup of tea or coffee at your convenience with the kettle provided. The flat-screen TV keeps you engaged with a variety of channels for your viewing pleasure.

Keep in touch with loved ones or catch up on work easily by staying connected with the free Wi-Fi. Enjoy a restful night's sleep with the roomy king bed, designed for your utmost comfort. Additional amenities include a refrigerator, allowing you to store refreshments, and laundry services for your convenience.

The Royal Suite also encompasses all the basic facilities you need for a comfortable stay, including a wardrobe, bedside table, and a private bathroom with essential amenities.

Discover a haven of luxury and tranquility in our Royal Suite, where every detail is carefully curated to provide an unforgettable experience.
